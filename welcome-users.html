Here's who we are and where we're headed.

Coast Charging Co started in late 2025 as a partnership between two friends, Kaleb and Steven. We had one idea that was really stuck in our heads: what if there were readily available e-bike charging stations around town? It doesn't make sense that you have to go home to charge up if you want to go further.

So we started dreaming about what a solar-powered alternative in the community could look like.

The name took a couple of tries. Kaleb was set on "Sunbrella" and even bought sunbrellaSD.com to build a prototype site. That plan ended when a lawyer for Sunbrella (the patio furniture company) sent over a cease and desist.

We landed on Coast Charging Co, tying ourselves to the ocean and to North County San Diego, the place that raised and shaped us. Think coasting down the 101 on your bike.

We finished our first prototype on June 7, 2026: all the electronics mounted on the back of a framed solar panel on wheels, running off a used e-bike battery. It could charge one bike at a time. A humble start, but it worked and gave us a platform to test software and different user experiences.

We're still a work in progress. Kaleb is designing a custom charge controller PCBA to manage charging profiles for multiple e-bikes at once, and Steven just built a new battery pack 2.5x larger than our original.

Our short-term goal is simple: build one station that can charge multiple e-bikes throughout the day.

Our ultimate goal: make solar-powered charging a normal part of local bike infrastructure, as ordinary as a bike rack, the Tesla Supercharger for e-bikes. When a charge is always nearby, range anxiety stops being a reason to drive instead of ride.

Thanks for being here early. We'll keep you posted as we build (with pics!).

Kaleb & Steven
